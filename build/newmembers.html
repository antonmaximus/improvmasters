Become a Member!

Joining means you can take on the various roles you see in our meetings: from being a functionary, to giving speeches and even running for office. It's 72 dollars for six months, and the first time you join a Toastmasters club, it costs an additional 22 dollars for the two books. The first book is the Competent Communicator, which has ten speech assignments designed to help you improve your speaking skills rapidly. You also get the Competent Leader manual which has assignments that help you build leadership skills.
